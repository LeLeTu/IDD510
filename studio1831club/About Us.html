ABOUT

Welcome To Our Studio

Studio 1831 is a yoga, fitness, and dance studio located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Studio 1831 offers yoga, belly dancing, and salsa lessons, as well as Zumba classes. We provide a clean, relaxing atmosphere in our yoga studio for you to enjoy the beauty of dance in. We hold daily and weekly classes and our full studio is also available for rent.

The Right Studio For You

Our Studio promotes dance as a way to achieve health and well-being. Providing energizing dance and fitness classes, we also offer relaxing massages to promote full body wellness. Let Studio 1831 ease your muscles, relieve your joint pain, and recover your energy, all while helping you enjoy a healthy, fit lifestyle.
