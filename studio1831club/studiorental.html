Studio 1831, located in the beating heart of the Art Museum neighborhood of Philadelphia, is an intimate performance and presentation space, available for rent for special events such as classes, lectures, workshops, performances, small concerts, and rehearsals.

  • Performance area is 15’x16’ (ceiling is 10’ high and ceiling fans are at 8’)
  • Seating is 50 folding chairs.
  • Basic lighting and stereo equipment

  • One restroom
