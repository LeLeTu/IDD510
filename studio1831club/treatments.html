Swedish Massage is a total body treatment that uses smooth flowing strokes to melt away tight muscles and release muscular and emotional tension throughout the body.

Swedish Massage typically involves the client being treated while lying on a massage table using essential oils to manipulate the muscles. Swedish massage has been known to alleviate join pain & fatigue,relieve sore muscles, and create a state of deep relaxation.

Deep Tissue Massage

Duration:

1 hour/$80

1.5 hour/$115

Deep tissue massage is a type of massage aimed at the deeper tissue structures of the muscle and fascia, also called connective tissue.

Deep tissue massage uses many of the same movements and techniques as Swedish massage, but the pressure will generally be more intense. It is also a more focused type of massage, as the therapist works to release chronic muscle tension and restriction of the fascia.

BENEFITS

  • Treats Chronic Back Pain
  • Helps Lower High Blood Pressure
  • Reduces Stress, Anxiety and Muscle Tension
  • Breaks Up Scar Tissue
  • Improves Athletic Recovery and Performance
  • Can Help with Labor Pain and Delivery
  • Reduces Arthritis Symptoms

Sports Massage

Duration:

1 hour/$80

1.5 hour/$115

Sports massage is a type of massage that is specifically tailored to sporting injuries or ailments.Sports massage focuses on specific areas of the body that require attention.

Sports massage with the use of hands, fingers, elbows, and forearms, the massage therapist will manipulate sore muscles and areas of injury to promote healing, relieve pain, and return flexibility to the joints. This massage is done while the recipient is fully clothed. Often used by athletes, this form of therapy helps maintain peak physical performance.

BENEFITS

  • Increased tissue permeability
  • Stretching
  • Break down scar tissue
  • Improve tissue elasticity
  • Pain reduction
  • Relaxation
  • Anxiety reduction
