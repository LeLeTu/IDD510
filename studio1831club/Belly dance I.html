Belly Dance I

About Belly Dance I

Belly dance is not only a way to improve one’s health,it is also the perfect way to reconnect with the intimacies of the soul.We’ve designed this class specifically for beginners and for those who want to brush up on basic technique. Students learn the basic moves most often seen in Cabaret/Egyptian style belly dance. Moves include shoulder and hip shimmies, hip drops and lifts, snake hips and figure eights,rib circles and rib drops, as well as basic dance posture and arm placement.
