The Art of Burlesque

You’ll start with a head-to-toe warm up followed by some hot exotic dance moves. Then you’ll add sexy floor moves to spice it up. Next you’ll learn some exciting moves across the floor.you’ll put it all together to create your own special dance. Once you’ve got your moves down we’ll dim the lights,give you a chance to let your hair down,and create your own exotic dance.
